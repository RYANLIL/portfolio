The following is a short excerpt of my life

I've traversed the twisted trails of Angular, glimpsed the depths of TypeScript, and conquered the peaks of JavaScript. My adventures have taken me through the wild frontiers of C# and .NET Core, the dangerous passes of ASP.NET. I'm no stranger to the backend, frontend, and everything in between.

I've connected to MySQL, wrangled MSSQL, and somewhere in between, tamed a wild MongoDB. My data journeys have led me through Schema after Schema, migrating and modeling, normalizing and indexing. I've optimized queries and tweaked tables, learned the secrets of stored procedures.

Through it all, I've used and abused every technology and framework that crossed my path. I've coded until dawn, refactored at midnight, and debugged my way out of many a digital dungeon. Though battered and weary, I stand tall, armed with experience and knowledge hard-won. The next adventure awaits!
-Ryan Lildhar
